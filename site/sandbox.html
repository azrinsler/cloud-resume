Stovetop Rice

Simple Recipes

Ingredients

  • Rice
    1 Cup
  • Water
    2 Cups
  • Better than Bouillon
    1.5 tsp

 

Items

  • Medium pot with lid
  • Something to stir with

Steps

  • Bring rice and water to a low boil in a covered pot over medium to medium-high heat.
  • Stir in the Better than Bouillon.
  • Reduce heat to low - keep covered.
  • Wait 10 to 15 minutes or until all water is absorbed.
  • Turn off the heat, stir, and re-cover.
  • Wait an additional 10 minutes or so for rice to cool.
